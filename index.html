About

I am a project management and operations professional with a Master of Science in Information Management from the University of Washington, a PMP certification, and an MPA. My work is centered on improving processes, aligning teams, and using data to better understand problems and support informed decisions.

Through my recent retail analytics project, I applied SQL, Snowflake, and Tableau to transform retail sales data into meaningful insights. The project reflects my interest in using data as a tool for solving business problems, improving workflows, and helping organizations make clearer decisions.

Featured Project

Enterprise Retail Analytics Pipeline

This project demonstrates how retail sales data can be transformed into useful business insights through a structured analytics workflow. I designed the project to simulate how an organization might use sales, customer, product, and store data to better understand performance and support decision-making.

The project includes data preparation, SQL analysis, Snowflake data modeling, and Tableau dashboard development. The goal was not just to build a dashboard, but to show how accurate, reliable data can help answer business questions and drive better business outcomes.
